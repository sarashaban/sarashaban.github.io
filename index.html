Maliheh Shaban Tameh, Ph.D.

About Me:

I am a Computational Scientist working at the intersection of Machine Learning and Materials Design, with over 10 years of multidisciplinary research experience spanning multi-scale simulations, quantum chemistry, machine learning, numerical modeling, and algorithm development. My expertise lies in developing and applying AI and machine learning algorithms to accelerate materials discovery. I excel at addressing complex, real-world problems through innovative modeling and analysis. As a collaborative team player, I thrive in dynamic, interactive environments and embrace challenging ideas that drive growth and innovation.
